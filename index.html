why alice?

Alice brings transparency to social funding

Alice is a social funding and impact management platform built on the Ethereum blockchain.

We incentivise social organisations (charities, NGOs, social enterprises) to run projects transparently, by making sure that they get paid more when they achieve their goals.

The performance of each project is publicly available, making it easier for funders (philanthropic organisations, impact investors, small donors) to identify and help scale social projects that actually work.

Shared impact data also helps reduce due diligence and reporting costs, and helps social organisations collaborate more effectively.

Join us to help eradicate the problems that these social projects are tackling.

Impact investing

Alice dramatically reduces transaction costs for impact investors, thanks to cheap & reliable due diligence, automated payments and a native secondary market.

Impact management

Alice decentralises the evaluation, monitoring and reporting of impact. This reduces administrative costs while making it easier to compare and benchmark projects.

Development Partners
IMPACT INNOVATION

with Social Alpha Foundation

Alice is working with SAF to foster new ways of delivering and tracking impact using blockchain technology. The partnership involves working with, and funding innovative non-profits that are either blockchain natives, or are leveraging the technology to improve people's lives in ways that haven't been possible before.
Secure & open ID

with Innovate UK & Imperial College

Mixing selective disclosure and obfuscation, Alice allows projects to collaborate by sharing information about the people they help, while respecting their privacy. Designed as an an open standard, compatible with all self-sovereign ID systems, and compliant with data protection regulations.
Impact Measurement

with the Impact Management Project

The Impact Management Project is a global, open and iterative initiative developing consensus about how to measure and manage impact. The shared fundamentals will be integrated deep into Alice’s impact management protocols to help grant-makers, impact investors and donors to compare and benchmark performance.
INTERNATIONAL EXPANSION

with CAF - the Charities Aid Foundation

The Charities Aid Foundation (CAF) is a global organisation that helps people and businesses support the causes they care about, and motivates society to give ever more effectively. CAF is collaborating with Alice on an operational level to transform lives and communities around the world.
