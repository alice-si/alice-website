OUR LIVE PRODUCT

Impact we track

Alice is a decentralised social impact engine built on the Ethereum blockchain. We help social organisations (charities, NGOs, social enterprises) produce transparent and reliable impact data.

We do that to increase the amount of money that social organisations can tap into, by solving the pain points of their would-be funders. 

That’s why we have two funding applications: one for donors (small donors, major philanthropists and grant-making bodies such as trusts, foundations and governments), and another for impact investors.

We allow social organisations to receive both philanthropic (live product) and impact investment funding. So as long as the social organisations prove their impact to their funders, they can scale their projects continuously.

Token sale summary

Alice tokens are ERC-20 compliant, and are used to pay network access:
a) Social organisations pay a fee to launch appeals
b) Investors pay to make and trade their investments
c) Users pay to access impact data for due diligence

A portion of all fees allocated as grants to social orgs, through a fund run by a DAO of Alice token holders.
