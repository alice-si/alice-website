what is alice donate

Impact we track

When you give to a charity on Alice, you can track whether it achieves its goals. In fact, you only pay if it does. This level of transparency is possible thanks to blockchain technology.

Transparent giving

Alice is all about being transparent and accountable to donors. That’s why Charities on Alice only receive donations if they achieve the goals listed on their project page.

Track your donation

Just click on “My Impact” in the menu bar to see how your donation has performed: how much has been received by the charity, and which goals it has paid for.

Cutting-edge technology

Alice is powered by “smart contracts” on the Ethereum blockchain. This ensures the traceability of your donation, and keeps funds secure until the charity’s goals are achieved.
