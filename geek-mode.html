Explore Alice's smart contracts

The code for our smart contracts is open sourced. We invite you to use it, and contribute to it, by visiting our Github page.The first charity appeal is live on Alice. You can monitor its progress on the blockchain:

- Donations: the main contract storing donations made to the London Street Impact: 15 Lives (LSI:15L) appeal
- Minting contract: this contract mints tokens representing the value of donations made in fiat
- Registry contract: this contract keeps track of donor balances and goals achieved

About our stablecoin partnership

When Alice first launched, we were one of the first companies in the world to use a regulated stablecoin. We participated in a pilot run by Tramonex Labs, within the Financial Conduct Authority's regulatory sandbox, to issue e-money (tokens that represent the value of fiat donations) on Ethereum. We are currently integrated with MangoPay to ensure the stability of donations, and are open to experimenting with other stablecoin solutions. Get in touch!
