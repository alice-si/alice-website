Full Stack Developer

About Alice

Alice is a blockchain-based impact network that helps identify and scale truly effective social and environmental projects. We’re hiring a smart, diverse and dynamic team of people that are inspired by the potential of technology to tackle the world’s most intractable problems.

If you have a tech, nonprofit, impact investment or cryptoeconomics background, and want to work with a passionate team of research and data-driven social innovators, please get in touch.

About the role

We’re looking for someone who wants to apply their technical skills to tackling complex social and environmental issues, and has a strong interest in the blockchain and building decentralised applications.

Responsibilities:

- Produce high quality code that is readable, documented and tested
- Own end-to-end delivery of functionality in a MEAN stack
- Research and implement platform scalability and performance improvements

Who we’re looking for:

- You have good knowledge of the Node.js ecosystem with practical experience of a web framework like Express
- You’re an expert in Javascript and have experience using Angular.js
- You have working knowledge of html, css/scss styling and responsive design
- You have experience with both NoSQL (preferably MongoDB) and SQL databases (e.g. MySQL)You have experience with developing a micro-services architecture by implementing small, modularised, interoperating components
- You’re used to working autonomously in a distributed and agile development team
- You aren’t phased by either deep technical challenges, or carrying out mundane tasks

Bonus points:

You frequent meet ups and are comfortable speaking to a community of developers
- You have devops experience
- You have experience with decentralised applications and open source projects
- You’re willing to travel from time to time

For more information on Alice, please visit our website, read our whitepaper and our blog, watch our presentations at Devcon3 and EthCC, or read the coverage about the launch of our first pilot.
