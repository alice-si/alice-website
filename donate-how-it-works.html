Impact we track

When you give to a charity on Alice, you can track whether it achieves its goals. In fact, you only pay if it does. This level of transparency is possible thanks to blockchain technology.

Alice is all about being transparent and accountable to donors. That’s why Charities on Alice only receive donations if they achieve the goals listed on their project page.
